Canon history

CONSUMER

Special service for consumers

Canon Korea Consumer Imaging Co., Ltd. is a Canon Korean subsidiary established with 100% investment from Canon headquarters, and has been in charge of official import of Canon digital optical products since its inception on September 13, 2005. We are doing our best to import, sell, and service Canon products so that Korean consumers can purchase and use better products faster.

DREAM & REALITY

We make dreams come true

Canon's pursuit of digital imaging is to express not only the visible, but also the invisible imagination and emotions, and empathize with anyone. With Canon's DSLR cameras, lenses, compact cameras, camcorders, and photo printers made with this vision, we are playing a role as a driving force that makes our customers' dreams a reality. Canon strives to provide new value to customers' lives through innovative services that exceed customer expectations and customized services for customer convenience.

PROMISE

Promise with society based on the ‘symbiosis’ ideology

Based on Canon Group's corporate philosophy of “contributing to the prosperity of the world and the happiness of mankind through the growth and prosperity of the company,” We aim to grow into a sound company that is respected by consumers. Based on this philosophy, we will strive to maintain good relationships with customers and local communities as well as the country, strive to protect the environment, and fulfill our social responsibilities.
